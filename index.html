About Myself

I am a My age year old student who is currently studying at the School of Information and Library Science at the University of North Carolina. I have aspirations to work in a library environment with a focus in technical services but I have experience in reference, circulation, and inter-library loan.

Early in my life I was fortunate enough to have the opportunity to shadow at Wake Forest University and explore the many possible avenues that existed in the library world. Since that opportunity I have volunteered at my public library, the Charles H. Stone Memorial Library in Pilot Mountain, worked as an undergraduate page at the Smith Library at High Point University and was fortunate to continue working there after my graduation; with a Bachelor of Arts in English Literature, as a reference and inter-library loan assistant.

I began my study at SILS in 2016 and plan to graduate in August of 2017 with a Masters in Library Science. For more information about my classroom experiences such as classes I have completed and projects completed please visit my Experience page.
